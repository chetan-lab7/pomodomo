How the Pomodoro Technique Works

1

Choose a Task

Select a task you want to complete and commit to focusing on it.

2

Set the Timer

Start the 25-minute focus timer and work without interruptions.

3

Work Until the Alarm

Focus completely on your task until the timer rings.

4

Take a Short Break

When the timer rings, take a refreshing 5-minute break.

5

Repeat & Track

After 4 focus sessions, take a longer 15-minute break.
